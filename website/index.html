About Palms n Sands

Experience the true essence of Goan hospitality at Palms n Sands, your ultimate beachside dining destination on the beautiful Candolim Beach. We specialize in serving the freshest catch from the Arabian Sea, prepared with authentic local spices and a touch of modern culinary flair. Whether you're craving a traditional Goan fish curry, succulent tandoori prawns, or a platter of grilled seafood, our menu is a celebration of coastal flavors. Join us for a memorable meal where the sound of the waves complements the taste of our exquisite dishes, creating a perfect seaside dining experience.

An Unforgettable Beachside Experience

At Palms n Sands, we believe a great meal is an experience. It's more than just food; it's the gentle sea breeze, the golden sunset, and the warm, friendly service that make moments unforgettable. Our restobar is designed to be your perfect escape. Relax on our upper deck for a panoramic view of the Arabian Sea, or choose a cozy spot on the sand for a romantic candlelit dinner. We are dedicated to creating a welcoming atmosphere where you can unwind with a craft beer, enjoy a signature cocktail, and make lasting memories with friends and family. Come for the food, stay for the vibe.
